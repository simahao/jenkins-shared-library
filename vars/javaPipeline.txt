A decomposed global variable with each individual build step encapsulated as a separate global variable.

<ul>
    <li>Checkout(multibranch support checkout automatically)</li>
    <li>Compile</li>
    <li>Unit test</li>
    <li>Quality analyze</li>
    <li>Package</li>
    <li>Builds image</li>
    <li>Publishes image to n3</li>
    <li>Runs any additional post build steps</li>
</ul>
